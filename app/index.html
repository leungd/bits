Bits

A simple SASS-based grid

Bits is an open-source SASS grid aimed to be as lightweight and simple as possible. The idea behind Bits was my own personal solution for smaller projects (I prefer SASS as my preprocesser) that don't require a full-fledged framework such as Foundation or Bootstrap.
